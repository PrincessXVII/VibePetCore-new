VibePetCore 2.6.14

What changed:
- Runtime pet update now caches static Bukkit entity state instead of reapplying it every pet-update tick.
- Pet custom names are refreshed only when the visible name signature changes.
- Pet social interactions now use a scan budget and rotating offset: small online is fully scanned, large online is capped to avoid O(n^2) work every second.
