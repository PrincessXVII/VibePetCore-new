# VibePetCore 2.6.23

## What changed
- Evolution items no longer trigger pet evolution through normal right-click feeding.
- Clicking an active pet with `NETHER_STAR`, `ECHO_SHARD`, or `DIAMOND` now shows a short hint and does not consume the item.
- Evolution remains available through the existing `/pet`, Source, and command flow with resource, stage quest, and chance checks.

## Server action
